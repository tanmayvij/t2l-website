Frequently asked questions

  • What is Tech2Learn?

    Tech2Learn is an online IT training portal for corporates, college students, organizations, school students and basically anyone who wants to upskill themselves in today's forever-changing world.

  • How to enroll for a course on Tech2Learn?

    Enrolling for a course is super simple! Just pick a course, click on Book Now, fill in your details and transfer the payment. And you're done! You will be added to the class and the details for the video conferencing will be conveyed to you by email.

  • Will I receive a Certificate of Completion?

    Definitely! All our courses are inclusive of a Certificate of Completion for you to boost your CV!

  • What will be the mode of teaching?

    All our courses will be conducted via live video conferencing on Google Meet.

  • I'm not great with technology - will I be able to learn online?

    Our experts will be with you every step of the way to help you out with any difficulty you might face. We assure you we'll make your experience with us as smooth as possible!
